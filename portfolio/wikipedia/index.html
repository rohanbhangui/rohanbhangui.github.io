Wikipedia Redesign (Concept)

So I started with custom glyphs and worked on 2 styles, one with upper case and one with lower case. I decided to move to less of a serif look for the font and more of a san-serif typeface.

Here I combined the two types of logos I had in the previous shot to form this one.

I ended up using the first concept I drew and iterated over it. The significance of the aqua stroke on the 'A' is that it shows that Wikipedia, and more importantly Wikimedia, is like a realm of knowledge. The stroke reminds me of a ring around a planet.
